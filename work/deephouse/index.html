Interactive Deep House Dance Projection

Having worked with TouchDesigner and the LeapMotion controller recently, I wanted to scale up my work both in terms of the input system and the output display. At its core, the project is an audio-and-motion-reactive visualizer as an interactive art. It focuses on the use of Deep House music as a genre for designing the visuals, including four procedurally generated patterns. The project is implemented using TouchDesigner with ZED-mini for depth sensing, and displayed using a projector. It was installed briefly in our lab for people to play around with.
